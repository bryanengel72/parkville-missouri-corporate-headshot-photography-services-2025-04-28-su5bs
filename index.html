Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services

What should I wear for my corporate headshot?

For corporate headshots, we recommend solid-colored business attire in neutral tones. Avoid busy patterns, bright colors, or trendy styles that may quickly date your photo. Dark suits with light shirts typically photograph well. We'll provide a detailed wardrobe guide upon booking.

How long does a typical session take?

Individual sessions usually last 45-60 minutes, including styling time. This allows for multiple poses and outfit changes if desired. Group sessions are scheduled based on the number of participants, with approximately 20 minutes per person.

What is included in the retouching process?

Our professional retouching includes skin tone evening, blemish removal, subtle wrinkle reduction, and color correction while maintaining a natural appearance. We also offer additional retouching services for specific requests.

When will I receive my final images?

You'll receive a private online gallery of proofs within 24 hours of your session. After selecting your preferred images, final retouched photos are delivered within 2-3 business days via secure digital download.

Do you offer group rates for corporate teams?

Yes, we offer special packages for corporate teams of 5 or more people. These packages include consistent lighting and backgrounds across all photos, plus volume pricing discounts. Contact us for detailed corporate rates.
