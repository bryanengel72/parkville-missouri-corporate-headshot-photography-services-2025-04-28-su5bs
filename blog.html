How to Prepare for Your Corporate Headshot Session: A Complete Guide

A professional headshot is a crucial investment in your personal brand and career. This comprehensive guide covers essential preparation steps, from wardrobe selection to posing techniques. Choose solid colors and classic pieces that complement your skin tone, and schedule haircuts 5-7 days before the shoot. During the session, maintain natural posture, angle your body slightly, and keep your chin forward for the best jawline definition. Technical considerations include arriving early and discussing specific requirements. Post-session, carefully review images for brand alignment and maintain both digital and printed copies. Regular updates every 2-3 years are recommended for optimal professional representation.
